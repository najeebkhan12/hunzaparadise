Welcome to Our Resort!

Camping in Luxury has become a new trend recently. We offer luxury stay with beautiful breathtaking views of Hunza Valley yet economical. Our Mission is to give you totally a new experience in an affordable way. Hunza Paradise is a luxury camping resort located at Duikar, Hunza Valley. We aim to provide comfortable accomodation with beautiful views of Hunza Valley in economical way. Hunza Paradise is an excellent camping site in Hunza Valley. We offer a unique experience of living in an authentic nomadic setting in a Safari Tent surrounded by towering glacier mountains.

Safari Tents

At 10m² each, they accommodate 2 persons (and possibly a child), furnished with beds, mattresses, pillows and linens, bedside tables, light and electricity. The most comfortable camping holidays you have ever experienced.
